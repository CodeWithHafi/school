Welcome Message

Fr. Bharat OMI

"Education is the powerful tool to change the world. At St. Eugene School, we strive to build character as well as careers."

A warm welcome to the virtual portal of St. Eugene English Medium School, Khatorbori. As you navigate through these pages, you will discover an institution that is deeply committed to providing holistic education rooted in faith, discipline, and modern values.

Following the footsteps of our Patron, **St. Eugene de Mazenod**, we are driven by the motto **"Striving for the Highest"**. Character formation is the foundation of our curriculum. We believe that true education is not just about scoring marks but about developing compassion, integrity, and resilience.

To achieve this, we blend tradition with innovation. Our SEBA affiliated curriculum is supported by modern **Smart Classrooms**, state-of-the-art labs, and a vibrant **extracurricular activities** program, including NCC, Karate, and Drawing. We aim to equip every student with the 21st-century skills they need to lead in a global world.

Education is a collaborative journey. I extend a warm invitation to parents to be active partners with us. Any official communication or appointment request can be addressed directly to me or the school administration office. Together, we can create a nurturing environment where our children can fly with the wings of their potential.

Wishing everyone a successful and fulfilling academic year. May St. Eugene School continue to shine as a beacon of knowledge and moral light in the Morigaon region.

Fr. Bharat OMI

Principal, St. Eugene School

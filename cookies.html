Cookie Policy

Understanding how we use cookies to improve your experience

To make the St. Eugene School website work properly, we sometimes place small data files called cookies on your device. Most big websites do this too. Cookies help our site remember your actions and preferences over a period of time, so you don’t have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website, such as the Principal's Message read-more section or Admission forms.

Performance & Analytics

We use these to understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g., which sections of our "About Us" page are most visited). This helps us improve the structure and content of our site for better parent-teacher communication.

Functional Cookies

These allow our website to remember choices you make, such as your preferred language or your location in Morigaon, providing a more personalized experience.

How to Control Cookies?

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish – for details, see aboutcookies.org. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit our site.

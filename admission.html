Mandatory Guidelines

  • Documents Required: Admissions are made only on the receipt of the Birth Certificate and Transfer Certificate.
  • Photos: Two recent passport-size photographs are strictly required.
  • Name Alteration: The date of birth and name certified at the time of admission will not be altered on any account later.
  • Transfer Students: Pupils coming from another school must bring a transfer certificate and mark sheet of the annual examination.
  • Fees: Annual fees must be paid with the tuition fee of the first month. Once paid, fees are not refundable.
Note

The Principal reserves the right to withhold results or deny admission based on school regulations.

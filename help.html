Visual guides to solve common problems for Parents & Students.

Admission Help

I want to enroll my child but don't know the process.

Steps:
1. Download Prospectus
2. Fill Inquiry Form
3. Visit Office (8:30am - 12pm)
4. Submit Documents

Fee Issues

I missed the 25th deadline or need fee details.

Steps:
1. Check Fee Book for dues
2. Pay at Bank/Office
3. Collect Receipt
4. Note: Rs. 10 fine applies after 25th.

Transport/Leave

My child is sick or I need to change the bus route.

Steps:
1. Write Leave Application
2. Email Principal's Office
3. Call Transport Head
4. Medical cert. needed for 3+ days.

Everything you need to know about admissions, academics, and life at St. Eugene.

You can apply by visiting our campus or through the online Admission portal. You will need to submit a birth certificate, transfer certificate (if applicable), and two passport-sized photos.

St. Eugene School is affiliated with the SEBA (Secondary Education Board of Assam). We focus on a balanced curriculum that integrates core academics with digital literacy.

Regular school hours are from 08:30 AM to 02:30 PM. On Saturdays, classes conclude at 12:00 PM. Note that the second Saturday of every month is a holiday.

School fees should be paid regularly before the 25th of each month. Late payments after this date may incur a fine of Rs. 10.

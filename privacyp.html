Privacy Policy

How we protect your data at St. Eugene School

Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to St. Eugene English Medium School. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our students, parents, and visitors. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website (seemskhatorbori.in).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information in several ways, including but not limited to:

  • Inquiry Forms: Name, email address, and phone numbers provided when you contact us.
  • Admission Portal: Student details, academic history, and guardian contact information.
  • Cookies: Anonymous data regarding your browsing behavior to improve website performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for educational and administrative purposes, such as:

  • Processing admission applications.
  • Sending school updates, newsletters, and emergency notifications.
  • Improving the user experience on our digital platforms.
  • Maintaining school records as per educational board requirements.

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement strict technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Personal data is stored on secure servers, and access is restricted to authorized administrative staff only.

4. Third-Party Sharing

St. Eugene School does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with trusted service providers (such as student management systems) or government educational boards when required by law.

5. Your Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about their child, request corrections, or ask for the deletion of data (where applicable by law). For such requests, please contact the Principal’s office directly.
